General Rotation

The Destruction Warlock playstyle is more of a priority list than a rotation. It will change a lot depending on the fight and the amount of Soul Shards you have.

The priority list is:
  • Apply Havoc if a secondary target is present
  • Maintain Immolate
  • Never cap on Soul Shards, make sure you cast Chaos Bolts at 4 Soul Shards in order to avoid waisting a Soul Shard. When playing Eradication you should focus on keeping as high uptime on the debuff as possible
  • Use Summon Doomguard/Infernal on CD
  • Use Grimoire: Imp on CD
  • Dont cap on Dimensional rift, use this when moving
  • Dont cap on Conflagrate stacks, use this to generate shards when low
  • Cast Rain of Fire on 4+ enemies when specced Soul Conduit. If not then cast on +5 enemies.
  • Life Tap if very low on mana or you have to move and have no Dimensional Rift stacks
  • Fill with Incinerate
Some other things that should be mentioned are that when playing Roaring Blaze you should always make sure you refresh your Immolate after it's had it's final tick, if not then you end up losing damage. Also make sure you have Conflagrate stacks ready for your next Immolate before buffing it at 24 seconds of duration. When playing Backdraft all you should really look after is that you dont mindlessly lose stacks while not capping stacks. Obviously it's going to be worth over-capping with a single stack if it means you'll be able to refresh a 3-stack buff you would have lost otherwise. Opener The Destruction Warlock opener really differs from fight to fight and also depending on what talents/legendaries you're using. F.ex. if you're using Lessons of Space-time you should obviously not dump all your charges of Dimensional Rift right after each other, but if you're not using them you might aswell. This means you should not always follow this 100%, but it should give you a general idea of how to open on a boss fight. Opener w. Backdraft
  • 4 seconds left on pull timer Life Tap (IF SPECCED)
  • 3 seconds left on pull use pot + pre-cast Incinerate
  • Cast Immolate to land on boss pull
  • Cast Summon Doomguard or Infernal
  • Cast Grimoire: Imp
  • Cast Soul Harvest aswell as any On-use trinkets/racials
  • 3x Dimensional Rift (Only cast 1x if you're using Lessons of Space-time)
  • Cast Channel Demonfire (IF SPECCED)
  • Conflagrate
  • Chaos Bolt
  • Maintain Immolate
  • Continue with normal rotation

Opener w. Roaring Blaze

  • 4 seconds left on pull timer Life Tap (IF SPECCED)
  • 3 seconds left on pull use pot + pre-cast Incinerate
  • Cast Immolate to land on boss pull
  • Cast Summon Doomguard or Infernal
  • Cast Grimoire: Imp
  • Cast Immolate again
  • Cast Soulharvest aswell as any On-use trinkets/racials
  • 3x Conflagrate (2x if no 4-set)
  • Chaos Bolt
  • Conflagrate when off-cd
  • Cast Channel Demonfire
  • 3x Dimensional Rift (Only cast 1x if you're using Lessons of Space-time)
  • Refresh Immolate at the last possible second to maintain the fully buffed Immolate on the target for as long as possible. Then refresh Immolate and wait until you have 3 stacks Conflagrate before you double-cast for a 24-second immolate and then repeat.
  • Continue with normal rotation
