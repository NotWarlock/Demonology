Heroic - High Tinker Mekkatorque

Eradication and Internal Combustion is better than Flashover and Reverse Entropy in theory if you are up for the added maintenance of Immolate and Eradication throughout the fight, but most opt to just use FO and RE.

This is a single target encounter with some damage immune adds. Flashpoint gains a ton of value on this fight since you can maintain Immolate on the spark bots nearly the entire fight. Since incoming damage is quite high and movement is minor throughout the encounter, either defensive talent will be more valuable than Burning Rush. As usual your azerite and trinket choices depend on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.

  • With Immolate on so many adds you will be casting Chaos Bolt more often and gives Eradication and Internal Combustion a ton of value on this fight.
  • A typical strategy for handling spark bots is to root them; roots have a damage threshold to break and since the spark bots take 99% reduced damage Agony will not break roots.
  • Gateway and Circle are both very useful for running Gigavolt Charges behind walls and returning to the group.
  • Imp is the pet of choice since the boss jumps around a lot.

Mythic - High Tinker Mekkatorque

The mythic version of this encounter is basically the same as heroic except the spark bots reflect damage via Spark Shield and Wormhole Generator can knock you up or polymorph you in addition to the normal teleport. Immolate will proc the reflect damage on you only when you first apply it. This means it's more of a risk to maintain Immolate for Flashpoint uptime but its still generally worth it to keep Immolate up on 1 Spark Bot if you can manage the damage coming in. Defensive talents are highly recommended for progress.

  • Circle should be used exclusively for Wormhole Generator in mythic as negating the fall damage is a huge boon.
  • Imp can dispel the Polymorph effect from Wormhole Generator.
