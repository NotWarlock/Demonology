Heroic - Jadefire Masters

Eradication and Internal Combustion is better than Flashover and Reverse Entropy in theory if you are up for the added maintenance of Immolate and Eradication throughout the fight, but most opt to just use FO and RE.

This is a two target stacked cleave encounter with some add spawns and you will spend most of the fight focusing Mestrah / Ma'ra to prevent her final phase. Rolling Havoc will be your best azerite trait for this fight, however whatever azerite traits sim best for 2 targets should be sufficient for this encounter. As usual your azerite and trinket choices depend on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.

  • Use Havoc on Manceroy / Anathose and continue to focus Mestrah / Ma'ra as you want these to die first.
  • When you are swapping to bombs and tigers, make sure you place Havoc on Mestrah / Ma'ra while focusing the adds down.
  • Shadowfury can be used to stun the tiger adds.
  • Use imp for this fight as the targets move around a lot and there is nothing to interrupt.
  • You can gateway over significant portions of the maze without getting knocked back, see the below image for placement (Horde only).

Mythic - Jadefire

The mythic version of this encounter is identical to heroic except that you are nearly guarenteed to be ported up to the Multi-Sided Strike and thus spend a decent amount of time unable to reapply dots to the boss.

  • All adds have more health in mythic so hard swapping while using Havoc on Mestrah / Ma'ra is likely necessary.
  • The order for the Multi-Sided Strike is always Blue -> Green -> Red -> Green -> Blue.

Available Loot

  • Gloves: Manceroy's Flamefists
  • Ring: Band of Multi-Sided Strikes
  • Helm: Blazewing Hood
