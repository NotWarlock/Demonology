Heroic - Conclave of the Chosen

Eradication and Internal Combustion is better than Flashover and Reverse Entropy in theory if you are up for the added maintenance of Immolate and Eradication throughout the fight, but most opt to just use FO and RE.

This is a single target priority burn encounter with some add spawns. There are always 2 bosses present until the end of the encounter however the second boss heals to full when the first dies so multidotting for damage is entirely useless although there is no downside to maintaining Immolate on all bosses for shards and Flashpoint uptime. This also makes Flashpoint by far the strongest azerite trait on this fight as you will have almost max uptime of the buff. Rolling Havoc is also extremely powerful for this fight as there is always something to havoc for stacks of this buff, but you may want to save Havoc for the raptors instead of using it on the boss As usual your azerite and trinket choices depend on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.

  • Make sure you use Havoc on the boss you are currently killing when you are swapping to raptors.
  • Shadowfury can be used to stun the raptor adds.
  • Make sure to refresh Immolate on the boss before add spawns to minimize dot downtime.
  • Imp has better target swap and can dispel Mind Wipe on other players (unless it bugs), Felhunter can purge Gift of Wind.
  • Gateway can be used to move across the room to Pa'ku's Wrath positions, 2 gateways will cover all possible spawns.
  • Circle should be placed in the middle of the room to reset after Paku and run from raptors.
