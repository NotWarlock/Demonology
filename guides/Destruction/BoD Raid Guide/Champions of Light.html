Heroic - Champions of Light

This is a fairly straightforward single target encounter with some spread adds. The adds that spawn will heal each other so are typically tanked together away from the boss. This allows you to funnel single target into the boss by maintaining Immolate on the adds and dumping CBs into the boss. Flashpoint is the best trait by far for boss damage. As usual your azerite and trinket choices depend on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.

  • Maintain Immolate on all adds for shards and Flashpoint uptime, it will not trigger Zealotry.
  • Face away from the crusader whenever he casts his ability or you will get disoriented; all your dots can be cast without facing your target.
  • Pet choice is largely irrelevant.

Mythic - Champions of Light

The mythic version of this encounter is basically the same as heroic with two simple changes; one of the adds throws hammers at the raid that must be dodged, and the healing adds cast Angelic Renewal that must be interrupted or the boss will heal to full.

  • Use felhunter pet and a focus or mouseover macro to easily interrupt the add cast (/cast [@focus,harm] Spell Lock)
  • You may need to manually switch targets with felhunter to interrupt if that adds are tanked too far away.
