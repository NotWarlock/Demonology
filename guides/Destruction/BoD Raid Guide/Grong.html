Heroic - Grong

Eradication and Internal Combustion is better than Flashover and Reverse Entropy in theory if you are up for the added maintenance of Immolate and Eradication throughout the fight, but most opt to just use FO and RE.

This is a single target encounter with a single add that spawns at ~20s and then every minute thereafter. Your usual highest simming single target setup will work best here. Crashing Chaos will be your best azerite trait for this encounter. Destruction is one of the best specs for killing the adds due to Havoc being up for every time the adds spawn. As usual your azerite and trinket choices depend on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.

  • Havoc the boss when the add spawn and focus kill the add.
  • Dark Pact almost perfectly aligns with the Tantrum / Death Knell and can help alleviate a lot of the healing, Demon Skin provides more overall absorbtion if Trantrums / Death Knells aren't causing issues.
  • Make sure to refresh Immolate on the boss before add spawns to minimize dot downtime.
  • Demonic Circle can be used to quickly get out of fire puddles or get back from running the throw out of the group.
  • Felhunter should be be used to assist with interrupts on the adds if needed, otherwise imp will provide the greatest target swap potential.

Mythic - Grong

The mythic version of this encounter is essentially the same as heroic except 2 identical adds spawn every 2 minutes rather than 1 add every 1 minute. The damage is also significantly higher and the fight is frequently 6 healed to alleviate incoming damage so the above statements about defensive traits and talents are more applicable in mythic.

  • Since there are 2 adds on mythic, make sure you Havoc the lower priotiy one and focus the one you are killing first
  • You will almost certainly need to assist with interrupts so Felhunter is by far the favorite pet choice.
